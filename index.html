Atmosphere

Atmosphere is a company that allows customers to customize headphones to fit their style. The logo represents customization with the use of different shapes and colors while the name, Atmosphere, refers to the music quality of the headphones. Along with the logo, I created the branding for stationary, an app, and a website.

Brow Know How

Brow Know How is a self-promotion piece that pokes a little fun at myself. It is a packaged sticker set of bracket shaped uni-brows that you can use on photographs or place any where you'd like. On the back of the package it tells a little bit about myself and tells how you can contact me. This is a fun way to introduce myself and is guaranteed to get a laugh out of anyone!

Three Dog Night LP

This is a redisgned LP for the albmun Seven Separate Fools by Three Dog Night.They are a band from the 70’s and their songs consist of nature, love, and freedom. I wanted to give a feeling of calmness and nature, but also give a modern look. The letters have the texture of a sunflower within and the tan background lets the colors pop from the flower.



National Constitution Center

The National Constitution Center needed help shedding light onto civic holidays and also bring in those customers who just aren’t visiting the center. The approach I took was to pull out the Philadelphia aspects of this center to attract locals. Using familiar imagery and sayings, I tried to catch locals’ attention. The posters represent three different civic holidays and include call to actions, the app allows you to read into what civic holidays actually are and how you can get involved, and the booklet includes information for locals about events nearby and the activities at the NCC.



On the Border

On the Border is a chain Mexican Restaurant that I created branding for. I included a fun paisley pattern in the logo to represent the traditional patterns used in Mexico, and I used natural materials to embrace the traditional Mexican culture.



Lily Yeh Artist Catalogue

Lily Yeh is an artist who creates beautiful mosaic murals. She started a non-profit organization with the mission to build a community through art. She involves children in her mural making and shows them how satisfying and fun it is to make art. She is a very humble and kind woman with a loud and creative imagination, this led me to create a colorful book with Lily's artwork shown full bleed. I created a mark for the front cover which is supposed to resemble a flower from one of her murals.



Deep Sea Dinners

This is a marketing app created for a company named Deep Sea Dinners that sells microwavable, pescatarian meals. Throughout this app I used fun illustrations of sea creatures with a clean and modern UI design. The app includes information about the pescatarian diet, what kind of meals they sell, and a survey.



Chess at Three

Chess at 3 is a company that teaches children from 3 years old and up how to play chess. They use fun stories and characters to help children understand the concept of chess. It is proven that when a child learns chess at an early age, it makes them better at problem solving, visual memory, better attention span, and several other improvements. Chess at 3 wanted to create a game set that they can give to children to learn at home that looks really fun and exciting. It includes a storybook, chess pieces, skins for the chess pieces, and a chess game board.



Proscape Website

This is a website redesign for the company Proscape, who provides an app making platform. I used a much more simplistic and modern approach, leaving the information and imagery to be the center of attention.



Ruffresh

Ruffresh is a hygiene company for dogs. They sell deodorizing spray in different scents for when your dog needs some "ruffreshing"! I created a fun illustration of a dog to create a playful and fun feeling that may remind dog owners of their pup.



MobileTechCreate

MobileTechCreate is an online publication that contains several articles. By using geometric shapes and a pop of some fun colors, I created a fresh and clean look while keeping the large amounts of information organized.



Girls Rock Philly

Girls Rock Philly’s mission is to empower girls from the Philadelphia area through music education that encourages fearless expression, leadership skills, and creativity. It’s a place where girls who may feel like they don’t fit in can feel totally comfortable and have a blast making music with friends. In my design, I wanted to appeal to the young girls who may be too intimidated to join this organization on their own. I stayed away from the loud, grungy look and steered toward friendly and super fun. All together I created two posters and a t-shirt.
